Our orphanage is a home to children who have lost their parents or caregivers. We provide them with a safe and loving environment, access to quality education, and the opportunity to participate in activities that help them develop their talents and interests. We also work to reunite children with their families whenever possible.
Our mission is to provide orphaned and vulnerable children with the care, support, and resources they need to thrive. We are committed to meeting the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of each child in our care, and to helping them develop into healthy and productive members of society.
Our vision is a world where every child has a safe and loving home, access to quality education, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.
